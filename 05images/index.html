Gee Related Entertainment

It's more than a lifestyle, it's a movement. Established in 2005 in southern California on the streets of LA's Florence District, Menace Loko's unique sound blends dirty south trap beats with old-school west coast, east coast, and down-south influences for the flavor that is Gee Related Entertainment (G.R.E).

It's More Than A Lifestyle, It's A Movement with more work than your neighborhood pusher. Menaceloko and Gee Related Entertainment are proud to present you with the soundtrack to the streets!
